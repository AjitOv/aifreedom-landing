Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Quick answers about our AI workshops, courses, and programs for schools, corporate teams, individuals and business owners.

What is the AI Awareness Workshop? +

A 1-hour interactive session for school and college students covering AI basics, live demonstrations of AI image generation, video creation and website building, hands-on activities, and career guidance for the AI era.

How long is a workshop? +

Our standard workshop is 60 minutes. Extended formats — 90 minutes or half-day deep-dives — are available on request for schools and corporate teams.

Do I need coding knowledge to learn AI with you? +

No. Every program — school workshops, corporate training, individual courses and business solutions — is designed for beginners with zero coding or technical background.

Is the demo session really free? +

Yes. Limited free demo sessions are available each month for schools and corporate teams. Book via our contact form or call +91 8007280721.

What do students learn in the school workshop? +

Students learn AI image generation, AI video creation, website building with AI, how apps are created, music generation with AI, and practical AI career guidance — all through live demos and hands-on activities.

What is included in the corporate AI productivity workshop? +

A hands-on productivity workshop teaching teams email automation, content creation, report and presentation generation, meeting notes, research and workflow automation — typically saving each team member 2–3 hours per day.

What AI courses are available for individuals? +

AI Basics — Zero to Hero (₹499), AI Prompt Engineering (₹699), AI Freelancing Masterclass (₹1,499), AI Se Kamao — Earn with AI (₹2,999), and AI Trading Mastery (₹2,999). All courses are beginner-friendly with no coding required.

How can AI help my business grow? +

AI automates marketing, content, lead generation, customer support and operations — typically reducing marketing costs by up to 50%, generating 3× more leads, and freeing business owners from repetitive manual work.
