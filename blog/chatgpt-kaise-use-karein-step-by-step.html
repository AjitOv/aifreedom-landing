ChatGPT Kaise Use Karein? — Complete Step-by-Step Guide (2026)

👨‍💻 Ajit (AI Finance Guy) 📅 Apr 28, 2026 ⏱ 11 min read

ChatGPT use karna sab ko aata hai. Lekin 10 mein se 9 log galat tarike se use karte hain — aur fir complain karte hain "AI accha jawab nahi deta." Sach yeh hai: output ki quality 100% prompt par depend karti hai.

Yeh guide aapko 11 minute mein ChatGPT pe expert bana dega. Account banane se le ke 6-part prompt formula tak — sab kuch step by step. Last mein 10 ready-to-copy prompts bhi hain jo aap aaj se use kar sakte ho.

💡 Is guide mein milega:
ChatGPT account setup · pehla prompt likhna · 6-part prompt formula · bad vs good prompt examples · 10 copy-paste prompts · 4 common mistakes · free vs Plus comparison.

Step 1: Account banao (2 minute)

  1. chat.openai.com kholo browser mein
  2. "Sign up" pe click karo
  3. Google account se sign up karo (sabse fast)
  4. Phone number verify karo (OTP aayega)
  5. Done. Free account mein GPT-4o-mini ka access milta hai — kaafi powerful hai daily kaam ke liye.

Mobile app bhi hai (iOS + Android) — same login. Voice mode bhi mil jata hai mobile mein.

Step 2: Pehla prompt likho

Ek text box dikhta hai. Aap kuch bhi likh sakte ho. Try karo:

"Mujhe Pune mein 5 days ka trip plan banao. Budget ₹15,000. 2 logo ke liye."

Enter dabao. ChatGPT ek detailed itinerary likh dega — day-wise plan, budget breakdown, sab kuch.

Ab follow-up sawal poocho:

"Aur sasta kar sakte ho? ₹10,000 mein adjust karo."

Yeh hai magic — ChatGPT context yaad rakhta hai. Conversation ki tarah baat karo.

Step 3: 6-Part Prompt Formula (game changer)

Yeh 6 ingredient har serious prompt mein dalo — output 10x better hoga:

  1. Role — ChatGPT ko bata "tum kaun ho". Example: "Act as a senior copywriter."
  2. Task — kya karna hai. Example: "Write a sales email for my AI course."
  3. Context — background information. Example: "My target audience is Indian college students. Course price ₹499."
  4. Format — output kaisa chahiye. Example: "150 words, friendly tone, with subject line."
  5. Examples — koi sample. Example: "Style mein Apple ki marketing copy chahiye."
  6. Constraints — kya nahi karna. Example: "Don't be salesy. No exclamation marks."

Step 4: Bad prompt vs Good prompt — live comparison

Same task, dramatic fark:

❌ Bad prompt:

"Mujhe email likh do AI course ke baare mein."

ChatGPT ka output: Generic, boring, sab ko same dega. Use karne layak nahi.

✅ Good prompt (6-part formula):

"Act as a senior copywriter. Write a marketing email for our AI Basics course. Target audience: Indian college students aged 18-22 interested in earning side income. Course costs ₹499, includes 10 video lessons, lifetime access. Format: 130 words, casual Hinglish tone, with one specific subject line. Use simple language a 16-year-old understands. Don't sound salesy or use exclamation marks. End with a soft CTA."

ChatGPT ka output: Specific, on-brand, conversion-ready. Aap copy-paste karke bhej sakte ho.

Same AI. Same model. Different prompt. 10x output difference. Yeh hai prompt engineering ka power.

Step 5: 10 ready prompts — copy-paste karo

Bilkul try karo abhi:

  1. Email shorter karo: "Make this email 50% shorter while keeping the same friendly tone: [paste email]"
  2. Meeting notes: "Summarize this meeting transcript in 5 action items with owners and deadlines: [paste]"
  3. Study notes: "Explain [topic] like I'm a Class 10 student. Use one simple Indian example. End with 3 quick questions to test me."
  4. Resume: "Rewrite this resume bullet point to sound impactful with metrics: [paste bullet]"
  5. Code explainer: "Explain what this code does line by line in simple Hinglish: [paste code]"
  6. Negotiation help: "I want to ask my boss for a 20% raise. Write 3 different opening lines I can use."
  7. Content idea: "Give me 10 viral hook ideas for an Instagram Reel about [your topic]. Hindi mein."
  8. Project planning: "I have 7 days to deliver [project]. Make me a day-by-day plan with hours."
  9. Translation: "Translate this English email to professional Hindi for a corporate audience: [paste]"
  10. Brainstorm: "I'm stuck on [problem]. Give me 10 angles to think about it from — surprising ones."
🎯 Pro tip: ChatGPT pe pin kar do aapke top 5 prompts. Saved Prompts feature use karo — har baar likhna nahi padega.

Step 6: 4 common mistakes — avoid karo

1. Hallucination — facts banane wala

ChatGPT confident sound karta hai galat baat bhi bolte huye. Specifically Indian facts, dates, government schemes mein woh galti karta hai. Hamesha verify karo kisi reliable source se.

2. Copy-paste in school assignments

Teachers AI text spot kar lete hain. Plagiarism checkers bhi behtar ho rahe hain. Use karo brainstorming + feedback ke liye, NOT to ghostwrite.

3. First answer hi accept karna

Pehla answer rarely best hota hai. "Make it simpler", "give example", "shorter version" — yeh follow-ups magic karte hain.

4. No context dena

"Write me an email" se "Write me a follow-up email after a 30-min sales call where the prospect asked about pricing" — context = 10x better output.
