## Organization Profile

**AI Freedom Institute** is an Indian EdTech organization providing practical, hands-on AI training across 4 segments:

1. **Schools & Colleges** — 1-hour interactive AI awareness workshops for students (Class 6 to college). Students learn AI image generation, video creation, website building, app creation, music generation, and career guidance.

2. **Corporate Teams** — AI productivity workshops teaching email automation, content creation, report generation, and workflow automation. Teams save 2-3 hours daily.

3. **Individual Learners** — Self-paced AI skills courses from ₹499. No coding required. Covers ChatGPT mastery, AI freelancing, content creation.

4. **Business Owners** — AI-powered marketing automation, lead generation, content creation, and operations optimization.
