Why use free AI tools for business?

Most small and medium businesses know AI is changing the game — but the path from "interesting" to "actually using it" is full of friction: paid subscriptions, complex onboarding, generic outputs that don't fit your business. That's the gap these tools fill. Each one is purpose-built around a specific business problem, takes inputs only you can provide, and gives you something concrete you can act on the same day.
