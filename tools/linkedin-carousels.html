🎨 Canva Execution Tips

A few rules to keep all 3 carousels visually consistent

Template: Search "linkedin carousel dark" in Canva → pick a 1080×1080 vertical-stack template.

Brand colors: Background #0a0a0f · Card #16161f · Accent green #4ade80 · Purple gradient #6A11CB → #2575fc

Font: Inter (matches your website) for body · Anton or Bebas Neue for hero headlines on slide 1

Slide 1 rule: Zero body text. Just the headline + arrow ➡️. Maximum impact.

Last slide rule: Always founder bio + soft CTA. Identical across all 3 carousels for brand consistency.

Export: PDF (LinkedIn auto-detects carousel format from PDF).

Posting: Best times for B2B India = Mon/Tue/Wed 11 AM-1 PM IST. Use the suggested time per carousel above.
