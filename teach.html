Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need a teaching certificate?

No. We care about your AI skill and how clearly you explain it. Past teaching experience helps but isn't mandatory.

How much can I earn?

Depends on format and effort. Per-session workshop rates are negotiated upfront. Recorded courses earn revenue share — your reach is the ceiling.

How much time commitment?

From 1 session a month to full-time. You set the pace. We won't force volume.

Can I teach remotely?

Yes. Online workshops, recorded courses and live cohorts are all remote-first. School/corporate in-person is optional based on your city.

Do you provide curriculum?

We share frameworks and templates. You bring your own teaching style and examples — that's what makes you valuable.

What if I want to leave later?

No lock-in. Your recorded courses can keep earning royalty as agreed, even if you stop taking new sessions.
