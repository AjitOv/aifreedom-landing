## Programs Overview

### 1. Schools & Colleges (/schools)

**Format**: 60-minute interactive AI awareness workshop
**Target**: Students Class 6–12, college students (all streams)
**Prerequisite**: None — no technical knowledge required

#### Workshop Curriculum

**Module 1: AI Basics (10 minutes)**
- What is Artificial Intelligence?
- How AI works in everyday life
- Types of AI: Narrow AI vs General AI
- Real-world examples students already use (YouTube, voice assistants, autocorrect)

**Module 2: Live AI Demonstrations (25 minutes)**
- **AI Image Generation**: Creating artwork from text prompts (DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion)
- **AI Video Creation**: Generating professional videos from scripts
- **AI Website Building**: Building a complete website in minutes without code
- **AI App Prototyping**: Designing and building apps with AI assistance
- **AI Music Generation**: Creating original music compositions
- **AI Writing & Research**: Academic writing, summarization, research

**Module 3: Student Interaction (15 minutes)**
- Students try AI tools hands-on
- Group activities and challenges
- Q&A with live tool exploration
- Problem-solving exercises using AI

**Module 4: Career Guidance (10 minutes)**
- AI career landscape in India and globally
- High-demand AI careers: AI/ML Engineer, Data Scientist, Prompt Engineer, AI Product Manager, AI Ethics Researcher, Robotics Engineer, Computer Vision Specialist, NLP Engineer
- Skills needed for AI careers
- Educational pathways and certifications
- How AI impacts non-tech careers (medicine, law, design, finance, agriculture)

#### Workshop Outcomes
After the workshop, students will be able to:
1. Explain what AI is and how it works in simple terms
2. Identify AI applications in daily life
3. Use at least 3 AI tools independently
4. Understand potential AI career paths
5. Think critically about AI's impact on society

#### Benefits for Schools
- **Innovation Leadership**: Position as forward-thinking, tech-savvy institution
- **NEP 2020 Compliance**: Supports digital literacy goals
- **Parent Satisfaction**: Parents value future-ready education
- **Student Engagement**: Interactive format students love
- **Zero Infrastructure**: Needs only projector + internet
- **Easy Scheduling**: 1 hour fits any school schedule

## AI Education Landscape in India

### Why AI Education Matters Now
- India aims to be a $1 trillion digital economy
- NASSCOM projects 1 million+ AI jobs in India by 2030
- NEP 2020 mandates computational thinking from Class 6
- 85% of jobs will be AI-impacted by 2030
- 65% of children entering primary school today will work in jobs that don't yet exist (WEF)

### Current Challenges
- Less than 5% of Indian schools offer any form of AI education
- Teacher training in AI is virtually non-existent
- Existing programs are too theoretical and coding-heavy
- Rural-urban digital divide limits access

### How AI Freedom Institute Addresses This
- **No-code approach**: Visual, interactive tools — not programming
- **Teacher-independent**: Our educators conduct sessions
- **Scalable model**: Workshop format reaches thousands efficiently
- **Multi-audience**: Schools, corporates, individuals, businesses — not just students
- **Affordable**: Accessible for all budgets
