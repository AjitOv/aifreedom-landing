FAQ

Corporate AI Training — Common Questions

Quick answers about our corporate AI workshops, formats, and pricing.

What is covered in your corporate AI training? +

Six practical modules: email automation, reports & presentations, content creation, meeting & task management, research & analysis, and workflow automation. Everything is hands-on with real AI tools — no coding required.

Do you conduct corporate AI training in Pune? +

Yes. We deliver onsite corporate AI training at offices across Pune — including Hinjewadi, Kharadi, Baner, Magarpatta, Viman Nagar and PCMC — and live online workshops for teams anywhere in India. See our dedicated Corporate AI Training in Pune page.

How long does the corporate AI workshop take? +

Standard sessions are 1–2 hours and fit around work schedules with zero downtime. Half-day, full-day and multi-session formats are available depending on your team's scope.

Does my team need a technical background? +

No. The training is designed for non-technical teams — HR, sales, marketing, finance, operations. Zero coding or technical background is required.

How much does corporate AI training cost? +

Pricing is custom-quoted based on team size, format (onsite or online) and scope. Start with a free discovery call — we understand your team's challenges first, then share a tailored quote.

What support do we get after the workshop? +

Every corporate workshop includes 30 days of post-training support plus implementation playbooks, so your team actually adopts the AI tools instead of forgetting them after the session.
