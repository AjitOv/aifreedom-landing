FAQ

Common Questions

Is coding required?

No. The entire program is built for non-coders. You'll work with visual AI tools and no-code automation (Make, Zapier, n8n). No programming required at any stage.

Is this beginner-friendly?

Yes. Week 1 brings everyone to a common baseline before we move into intermediate tools and project building. If you can use a smartphone confidently, you can complete this program.

Online or offline?

Hybrid. Most sessions are live online so anyone in India can attend. Select workshops + cohort meet-ups are offline. Recordings cover everything you miss.

Will I get recordings?

Yes — on Professional and Master Trainer plans. Lifetime access, replayable on any device.

How does the certification work?

You complete weekly mini-projects and a Day-30 live capstone (a 60-minute AI workshop you deliver). After review, you get the industry-oriented AI Trainer certificate — LinkedIn and resume-ready.

Can I become a trainer right after this?

That's the whole point of the program — the last week is dedicated to workshop design, pricing, client acquisition, and your own offer. Most Master Trainer alumni run their first paid workshop within 60 days of completion.

What's the refund policy?

Free Career Call before any payment. After enrollment, 7-day refund window if you've completed less than 25% of the program.
